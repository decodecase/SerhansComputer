Date: 1 March 2024
Subject: Just listen

Kenan,

You’ll hear it in the second movement. I didn’t use your words exactly — I translated them. Into the only language I have left.

I gave you credit in the way I know best.

You always said silence speaks.  
Well, this time it sings.

– S
